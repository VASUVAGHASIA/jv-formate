## ğŸ¨ Formatting Details

### Fonts
Available professional fonts:
- **Calibri** - Modern, clean (default for business)
- **Arial** - Universal, professional
- **Georgia** - Formal, traditional (great for reports)
- **Times New Roman** - Academic, legal
- **Verdana** - High readability (manuals, guides)

### Spacing
- **1.0** - Tight, compact
- **1.15** - Standard Word default
- **1.5** - Professional reports
- **2.0** - Academic, legal documents

### Margins
- **Normal**: 1" all sides
- **Narrow**: 0.5" all sides (newsletters)
- **Moderate**: 1" top/bottom, 0.75" sides (business)
- **Wide**: 1.5" left (manuals for binding)

### Alignment
- **Left** - Standard documents, easy reading
- **Center** - Titles, headers
- **Right** - Headers, specific elements
- **Justify** - Formal reports, professional documents
