## üß† Smart Content Structuring

### When to Use PARAGRAPHS (Justified/Left)

‚úÖ **Use paragraphs for**:
- Explanations and analysis
- Narrative and storytelling
- Arguments and reasoning
- Background and context
- Conclusions (narrative style)
- Legal/academic content

**Example**:
> Artificial Intelligence (AI) has emerged as a transformative force in modern healthcare, revolutionizing diagnostics and treatment planning. The global AI healthcare market reached $19.8 billion in 2024, with projections indicating growth to $187.95 billion by 2030.

---

### When to Use BULLET POINTS (Left-aligned)

‚úÖ **Use bullets for**:
- Lists of items or features
- Key takeaways and highlights
- Benefits and advantages
- Requirements and specifications
- Action items (non-sequential)

**Example**:

> ‚Ä¢ **Improved Accuracy**: 40-60% reduction in diagnostic errors
> ‚Ä¢ **Faster Processing**: Analysis time from hours to minutes
> ‚Ä¢ **Cost Efficiency**: 30-50% operational savings

---

### When to Use NUMBERED LISTS

‚úÖ **Use numbered lists for**:
- Sequential steps or procedures
- Ranked items (priority order)
- Chronological events
- Legal/formal sections

**Example**:

> 1. Download the installer
> 2. Run setup.exe as administrator
> 3. Follow the on-screen instructions
> 4. Restart your computer

---

### When to Use TABLES

‚úÖ **Use tables for**:
- Comparing 2+ options
- Data presentation (statistics, metrics)
- Specifications and parameters
- Schedules and timelines
- Pros & cons side-by-side

|---------|-------|-----|------------|

## üìê Alignment Rules

### Justified Alignment (Professional)
‚úÖ Use for:
- Technical reports (formal)
- Research papers
- Legal documents
- Business proposals (body text)
- Academic essays

**Why**: Professional, publication-quality appearance

---

### Left Alignment (Standard)
‚úÖ Use for:
- Casual documents
- Newsletters
- Emails and memos
- Bullet point lists (always!)
- Headings (always!)
- Modern business docs

**Why**: Easier to read, modern look

---

### Center Alignment (Minimal Use)
‚úÖ Use ONLY for:
- Document title (main title only)
- Cover page elements
- Standalone quotes/callouts

‚ùå NEVER center:
- Regular paragraphs
- Headings (except title)
- Body text
